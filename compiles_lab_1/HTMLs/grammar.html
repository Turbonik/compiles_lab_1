Грамматика G[Start]

1) <Start> → 'const ' <val>
2) <val> → 'val ' <id>
3) <id> → letter <idrem> | '_' <idrem>
4) <idrem> → letter <idrem> | digit <idrem> | '_' <idrem> | ':' <type>
5) <type> → 'Int' <equal>
6) <equal> → '=' <befnum>
7) <befnum> → '-' <afterminus> | nonzerodigit <intrem> | '0' <afterzero>
8) <afterminus> → nonzerodigit <intrem>
9) <afterzero> → ';'
10) <intrem> → digit <intrem> | ';'
11) <digit> → '0' | '1' | ... | '9'
12) <nonzerodigit> → '1' | ... | '9'
